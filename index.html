What does eat clean mean?

Put simply, clean eanting is avoiding all processed food, relying on fresh fruit, vegetables and whole grains.

How can I eat clean?

Clean eating is simple. Choose whole foods and minimize eating highly processed and unrefined foods, including most fast food places. Include plenty of fiber, good fats and protein in every meal to help you maintain full throughout the day.
